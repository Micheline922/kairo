# **App Name**: Sanctuary

## Core Features:

- Secure Inner Room: Login system using Firebase Auth with biometric lock (FaceID/Fingerprint) entry after login. Encrypted data for privacy.
- The Living Word (Bible Module): Integration of a multi-language Bible API to highlight verses and save 'Pearls of Wisdom'.
- AI Semantic Search: Search for verses based on feelings and context, not just keywords.
- The Spiritual Journal (AI Companion): Editor for writing spiritual reflections. AI analyzes the 'spiritual climate' of the text and provides a 'Word of Light'.
- Project/Doubt Space: AI acts as a biblical mentor to help discern God's will for specific decisions; the LLM will use a Bible-oriented tool in making its decisions.
- The Fasting Altar (Fasting Planner): Tool to program fasts (Duration, Type, Purpose).
- AI Sustenance: AI generates a customized reading plan for the fasting period based on the specific 'Why' of the user, with real-time progress bar and 'Strength Verses' notifications.
- The Academy (Modern Wisdom): Concept vs Word search bar. AI explains modern terms by bridging the modern world and biblical truth, explaining what God expects from us in that specific context; the LLM will use a knowledge retrieval tool.
- Firestore Integration: Utilize Firestore database to store and manage user-generated content such as journal entries, saved verses, and fasting plans.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3B5998), reminiscent of classical iconography of divinity and truth.
- Background color: Cream (#F8F5F0), offering a soft and serene backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700), to highlight important elements and add a touch of divinity and wisdom.
- Body and headline font: 'Playfair', serif, Display for a classic and high-end feel. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use minimalist and elegant icons, such as feathers, crosses, and doves, to represent faith and spirituality.
- Clean and uncluttered layout, prioritizing white space to create a sense of peace and reflection.
- Subtle animations and transitions to create a smooth and engaging user experience.
